Privacy Policy

Effective date:

This Privacy Policy describes how the BetMate mobile application (the “App”, “we”, “our”) handles information when you use it. By installing or using the App you agree to this policy.

Short version: we do not require an account, we do not collect personal information directly, and we do not sell any data. The App displays publicly available betting market data and shows ads through Google AdMob.

1. Information we collect

Information you provide

None. The App does not require sign-up, registration, or any input of personal information.

Information collected automatically

When you use the App, the following technical information may be processed locally on your device or by third-party services we integrate (see Section 3):

  • Approximate IP address (used by Supabase, our hosting provider, for routing and abuse prevention)
  • Device identifiers and ad-related signals (used by Google AdMob to show non-personalised or personalised ads)
  • Cached betting market data stored locally on your device for offline access (no personal data inside)

2. How we use information

We use the limited information described above only to:

  • Operate the core function of the App (display predictions and market data)
  • Cache content locally so the App remains usable offline and faster on subsequent launches
  • Display advertising via Google AdMob
  • Diagnose and fix bugs (technical logs may be reviewed if you contact support)

3. Third-party services

The App integrates the following third-party services. Their own privacy practices apply:

  • Google AdMob — serves advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers, IP address and ad interaction data. See Google’s ads policy and Google’s privacy policy.
  • Supabase — hosts the read-only data the App downloads (predictions and the daily market summary). See Supabase privacy policy.
  • Public betting market data sources — the analytics displayed are derived from publicly available aggregate market information. The App does not transmit any of your personal data to these sources.

4. Data we do not collect

We do not collect, store, or process:

  • Your name, email, phone number or postal address
  • Payment or financial account details
  • Precise location (GPS)
  • Contacts, photos, calendar, or any device content unrelated to the App
  • Health, biometric, or government identifier data

5. Local storage on your device

To work offline and reduce data usage, the App caches the latest market snapshot in a local file (or browser local storage on the web version). This cache contains only public market figures — never personal data. You can clear it at any time by clearing the App’s storage from your device settings.

6. Children’s privacy

The App is intended for users aged 18 or older. It is not directed to children. We do not knowingly process information from children. If you believe a minor has used the App, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

7. Your rights

Depending on where you live (e.g. EU/EEA under GDPR, California under CCPA), you may have rights regarding personal data. Because we do not knowingly hold personal data tied to you, most requests will be informational. To exercise any right, contact us at the address below.

  • Access — request what data (if any) we hold about you
  • Deletion — ask us to delete any data tied to you
  • Opt-out of personalised ads — via your device’s “Opt out of Ads Personalisation” setting

8. Security

All network connections from the App use HTTPS. The Supabase backend is access-controlled. No system is perfectly secure, but we make reasonable efforts to protect the limited data the App handles.

9. International transfers

Supabase data is stored in the EU region. Google AdMob may process data globally according to its own infrastructure.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Effective date” at the top will reflect the latest version. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance.
